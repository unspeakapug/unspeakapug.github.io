Puggy Bot

Discord Bot — Online
Realm Bot — Online
Beta Bot — Online

The Puggy Bot is a Discord bot designed to make your experience better. Its features range from fun to utility, making it useful for even more people. Theres also a Beta Bot with features that aren't ready to be added to the primary bot yet.

Notes: Some features require specific permissions in your Discord server or Minecraft Realm. If the permissions aren't given the bot will not function as intended. Another additional note, if the bot Terms of Service is broken you could be blacklisted. This means you will be unable to use the bot.

Main Features

  • /Doog, /Doogbomb, and /Doogquote — commands related to The Doog
  • Pug Points and scoreboard/leaderboard features to compete with others
  • /rewards, /buildings, and /boost — commands providing even more Pug Points to speed up progression
  • /chatbot — a Minecraft Realm to Discord server relay
  • /xtictactoe and /xblackhack — game style commands that give rewards
