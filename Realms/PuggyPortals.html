Realm Portals

Explore the various portals in the Puggy Realm hub! Each portal leads to a unique area with its own features.

Main Portals

  • Shops

    Sell ores and purchase hundreds of items from kits to mayflies and ranks!

  • Misc Areas

    Explore a variety of smaller areas without their own portal.

  • Plots

    Explore peoples builds and homes. Purchase a plot in the Normal Shop!

  • Private Smelters

    Small rooms with furnaces, crafting tables, and kit access without risking items being taken by others.

  • Rank Areas

    Access special areas requiring ranks with exclusive items.

Smaller Portals

  • Chill Area

    A floating island connected to spawn used to relax and hang out with other players.

  • Library

    View a variety of books written by members/staff and apply your book to be added as well!

  • PvP Arena Redesigned

    PvP against other players in a specially designed arena!

  • Mob Arena

    Fight mobs and collect their drops.

  • Puggy SMP New Map

    Explore a survival-style SMP world and build freely.
