Puggy Bot Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Puggy Bot collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with the Bot. By using the Bot, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Discord User Information: Your Discord username, user ID, and any messages or commands you send to the Bot.
  • You must not use the Bot for any illegal or harmful activities including harassment, spamming, or violating TOS.
  • Minecraft and Xbox Information: Your UUID, XUID, and any other Xbox information is collected for specific features like the Relay. We do not collect any personal information connected to your Xbox account like your real name.

The Bot does not collect any personally identifiable information beyond what is required for its functionality.

How We Use Your Information

  • Providing Bot Services: To respond to user commands and improve the Bot's functionality.
  • User Interaction: To process user interactions such as commands or events in Discord servers.
  • Improvements: To analyze how users interact with the Bot and enhance features.

Note about the recent RK/Puggy Merge: All data remains private to Puggy Bot developers. Everyone who has become staff from the merge does not have access.

Data Retention

  • Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services.
  • Messages and commands are processed in real-time and discarded unless needed for features like tracking points or rankings.

Data Security

  • We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Rights

  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting the head staff or owner in the Puggy Discord.
  • You may request a copy of any data the Bot has collected about you.

Third-Party Services

  • The Bot may interact with third-party APIs (e.g., Xbox API) to function. Review their privacy policies as we do not control them.

Changes to Terms

Updates to this Privacy Policy will be announced in the official RK and Puggy Discord servers.

Contact Us

For questions, reach out in the RK Discord.

Extra - FAQ

Q: What data does the Bot store?

A: The bot only stores information all people could get such as Discord user IDs, usernames, messages/commands, and Xbox/Minecraft IDs. Despite everyone being able to get it, it is still kept private in our bot.

Q: Can I get my data cleared?

A: A little. Logs of you using the bot and all related information will not be deleted for safety purposes. Information such as your Discord user ID, username, and messages/commands are kept for the bot's functionality. If you'd like your information cleared please contact a Puggy Bot developer.

Q: Who has access to my data?

A: Only Puggy Bot developers. Nobody else including staff members who aren't devs.

Q: Does the Bot share my data?

A: No, the Bot never shares, sells, or distributes your information away to anyone.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes. Measured are taken to keep your information secure but unfortunately nothing is 100% secure. We put in a lot of effort to keep your information secure.
